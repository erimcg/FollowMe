CIS-450 Software Engineering is taught by Dr. Eric McGregor. We were divided into four separate teams depending on our talents and tasked with making an android application for Bridgewater College's Biology department to use. The application development team is in charge of making and developing the application. The quality control team is in charge of making sure the app works well and errors are fixed. The web development team fixes and builds a website to pair with the app. Within the web development team is the database administrator team. The planning team communicates with the clients and confers their wants to the team. Each team has a manager and a there is a lead manager who oversees the main managers. The application tracks the users movement by global positioning system so others can follow and observations can be added along the path. The user can toggle whether they want others to be able to change their path and add observations too it. The user can also toggle between walking the path or driving it.
